We are based in Barcelona but most of our events happen somewhere else. A centralised agency with a mobile roster works better than a local DJ for one simple reason: consistency.

The local DJ trap

International couples planning a wedding in a Spanish region they have never visited usually try to book local. It makes sense on paper, the DJ knows the venue, the sound engineer, the noise curfew. In practice, this is where most regrets start. You cannot vet a local DJ from another country. Reviews are sparse. Communication is awkward. And their range may not match what your guests expect.

Why a Barcelona base works for the whole country

Barcelona has the deepest pool of international DJ talent in Spain. The city attracts musicians from every continent, and the best of them travel constantly for weddings, brand events and private parties. Our roster regularly plays Madrid, Valencia, Sevilla, Bilbao, San Sebastián, Costa Brava, Costa del Sol and the islands. The DJ arrives fully prepared, with the same kit, the same reliability and the same quality as if they were playing in Barcelona.

Practical advantages of a centralised agency

  • One point of contact, one standard of quality, regardless of where your event is
  • DJs who have played hundreds of events know how to handle a new venue quickly
  • Standardised equipment and backup plans, no local surprises
  • Transparent pricing including travel, you do not get hit with surprise costs
  • English-speaking coordination from first contact to the last song of the night

Where we have played across Spain

Barcelona and the Catalan coast are our home, but our DJs regularly play events in Madrid, Valencia, Málaga, Marbella, Costa Brava, San Sebastián, Sevilla and villa events in the countryside of all of these regions. If your wedding or corporate event is anywhere in Spain, we can cover it.
