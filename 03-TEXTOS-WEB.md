# 6. FAQ

## Section title
```
Questions
```

---

**Q: How much does it cost?**
```
Our standard wedding package starts at €1,000 for a full reception
(cocktail through party).
Final pricing depends on duration, location, and specific requirements.
We'll give you a clear quote after the matching process.
```

---

**Q: How far in advance should I book?**
```
For peak season weddings (May–October), we recommend reaching out
at least 6 months in advance.
But if you're closer to the date, still get in touch—we might have availability.
```

---

**Q: Can I meet the DJ before the wedding?**
```
Yes. Once matched, you'll have a video call with your assigned DJ
to align on the vision, music, and timeline.
```

---

**Q: Do you provide sound equipment?**
```
We provide DJ equipment.
Sound systems (PA, speakers) are typically arranged through your venue
or production team. We can advise or connect you with trusted partners.
```

---

**Q: What if I don't know what music I want?**
```
That's exactly why the matching system exists.
You don't need to know genres or artists—just describe the vibe,
and we'll translate it into the right DJ for you.
```

---

**Q: Do you work outside Spain?**
```
Yes. We're based in Spain but available across Europe
for weddings and events. Travel fees may apply.
```

---

**Q: Can I send a playlist or requests?**
```
Absolutely. Your DJ will work with your input.
But we also trust our DJs to read the room and adapt in real time.
The best parties are a balance of both.
```
