( 03 ) STORY WHO WE ARE

DJs who knew
what clients
really needed.

We are a group of DJs with years in the industry. We have played weddings, galas, corporate events and private parties across Spain and beyond. Over time, a pattern emerged from every client conversation: finding a DJ you could truly trust was genuinely hard.

Clients told us the same thing over and over. They had gone through agencies, scrolled through endless profiles, made calls across time zones, and still arrived at their event not really knowing what they would get. For couples who care deeply about music, about the energy of the night, about their guests dancing until the venue closes, this uncertainty was unacceptable.

So we built elevn. Not as a directory, but as a matchmaking service. We use everything we know about music, crowds and events to put the right DJ in front of the right client. Every time. No surprises. No going in blind.

Based in Barcelona. Spain's premier destination DJ agency for international clients.
