( 04 ) FAQ COMMON QUESTIONS

Questions
about wedding DJs.

How do I find a good wedding DJ in Spain?

ELEVN DJS is a boutique DJ agency based in Barcelona that specialises in matching international couples with curated wedding DJs across Spain. You fill out a short questionnaire about your taste, crowd and venue, and we assign the DJ whose style fits your wedding. No guesswork, no compromises.

How much does a wedding DJ cost in Spain?

Wedding DJ prices in Spain vary depending on the location, duration and specific requirements. ELEVN DJS provides personalised quotes after understanding your event. Contact us for a tailored proposal within 48 hours.

Do your wedding DJs speak English?

Yes. Every DJ on our roster is fluent in English and experienced with international weddings. Many also speak Spanish, French and other languages, making them ideal for multicultural celebrations.

Where in Spain do your wedding DJs work?

Our DJs are based in Barcelona and work across all of Spain, including Madrid, Ibiza, Mallorca, Marbella, the Costa Brava, Sitges and more. We cover destination weddings anywhere in the country.

Can I meet my wedding DJ before the event?

Absolutely. Every booking includes a pre-event call with your assigned DJ to align on the brief, key songs, special moments and anything off-limits. You will feel confident going into your wedding day.
