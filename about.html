About ELEVN

A boutique DJ agency based in Spain, specializing in modern events and international celebrations. We exist because finding the right DJ shouldn't be a guessing game.

We're not a booking platform. We're a curated selection.

ELEVN is a boutique DJ agency based in Spain, built for modern events and international clients.

We're not a sound company. We're not a DJ collective. We're a curated selection of DJs with a system built for quality.

We handle the selection. You enjoy the party.

Every event gets personal attention. Every DJ is briefed specifically for your celebration. We translate your taste, your vibe, and your vision into the perfect set.
